Ligature Design CO

Ligature Design is a multidisciplinary design studio based in Portland, Oregon, that specializes in branding, web design, and user experience. The studio was founded in 2012 by partners Grant Greer and Joann Richards. We have a passion for design that is evident in our work. We strive to create simple, clean, and effective designs that communicate clearly and effectively.
